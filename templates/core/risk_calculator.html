Position Sizing
Position sizing determines how many units to buy or sell. The formula is:

Units = (Balance × Risk%) ÷ Stop Distance
This ensures every trade risks the same percentage of your account, not the same dollar amount.
Risk : Reward Ratio
R:R compares potential profit to potential loss. A 1:2 R:R means you risk $1 to make $2.

❌ Below 1:1 — avoid these trades ⚠️ 1:1 — breakeven at 50% win rate ✅ 1:2+ — profitable even at 40% win rate 🏆 1:3+ — professional standard
Drawdown Protection
Losing streaks are inevitable. Protect your capital:

At 1% risk — 10 losses = 9.6% drawdown At 2% risk — 10 losses = 18.3% drawdown At 5% risk — 10 losses = 40.1% drawdown At 10% risk — 10 losses = 65.1% drawdown
